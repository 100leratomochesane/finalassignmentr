TIPS TO CONCENTRATE IN CLASS

  1. Remove distractions, like your phone.

    Distractions could be a computer, a phone, small items you could play with, something around you
    like your friend or a window.

  2. Prepare before class to stay focused on the lesson

    Being in the right mindset before class can help you pay attention. Before class starts, try looking
    over your homework and reading the previous day's notes. This can get your brain into "class mode," making it easier to focus.

  3. Remove distractions, like your phone.

    If you are having a hard time focusing, try changing where you sit. A new space and environment
    can help you focus more on the lesson, especially if the old spot was full of distractions. So opt for a seat in the front to an eye on the teacher and board. Studies show that students who sit in front have better grades on average.
